Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Ask Samer collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use this website and when you book and pay for a 1:1 call.

1. Data Controller

Ask Samer
Email: contact@asksamer.de

2. Data We Collect

We collect and process only what is necessary to provide our services:

  • Website usage data (via privacy-friendly analytics)
  • Booking data (via Calendly)
  • Payment data (via Stripe)
  • Any information you voluntarily provide during communication or the call

3. Website Analytics

This website uses Simple Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics tool.

Simple Analytics does not use cookies and does not collect personal data such as IP addresses.

For more information, visit: https://simpleanalytics.com

4. Booking and Payment

When you book a call, you are redirected to Calendly. Calendly collects your name, email address, and booking details.

Payments are processed through Stripe. Stripe may collect payment information such as card details, billing address, and transaction data.

We do not store your payment information on our servers.

5. Legal Basis

We process data based on:

  • Your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR)
  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR)
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR)

6. Data Sharing

Your data is only shared with third-party services that are required to run this website and provide bookings/payments (Simple Analytics, Calendly, Stripe).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your rights, contact:

contact@asksamer.de

9. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
